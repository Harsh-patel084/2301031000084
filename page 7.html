1. NASA's "Eyes on the Solar System" Expands to Include New Data
Date: January 2025
Update: NASA's Eyes on the Solar System tool has added new features, allowing users to explore a growing range of celestial bodies in greater detail. This interactive platform now includes data from recent space missions, like the James Webb Space Telescope, and real-time updates on missions like Perseverance on Mars. Users can explore detailed 3D models of planets, moons, and spacecraft from NASA's missions.
