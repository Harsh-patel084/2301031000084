2. Virtual Reality (VR) Expands to Space Exploration

Date: December 2024

Update: Space agencies and private companies are increasingly turning to virtual reality to bring space exploration closer to the public. Platforms like Space Engine and Apollo 11 VR are gaining attention for their ability to immerse users in space. For instance, the NASA VR Experience now allows users to tour the International Space Station (ISS), or ride along on the Perseverance Rover as it explores Mars.
