Mars Orbiter Mission (Mangalyaan): ISRO's Mars Orbiter Mission (MOM), also known as Mangalyaan, captured stunning images of Mars and has made the exploration of the Red Planet more accessible. The mission's images and data are often shared on online platforms where you can view the surface of Mars, including features like Olympus Mons (the largest volcano) and Valles Marineris (a vast canyon system).

ISRO may also host virtual experiences or offer 3D maps based on the data collected from the mission. These allow you to explore the Martian surface and study its geological features.
